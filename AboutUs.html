About us

Watson Productions is a company based in Mount Airy, North Carolina that sales The Model Cap. They feature embroidery that shows birth year in the same
style that you would represent the year that a car was manufactured. These hats are commonly sold in Virginia and Tennessee at restaurants such as Shoney's, Ihop,
many pancake houses, and even Smokey Mountain Knife Works. The company has been in business since 2004 created by Jerry Watson and was taken over by
its new owner Kerry and Melinda Cummings in 2018. You can also contact the owner through the contact us page
featured here on the website to get in touch for finding a certain year, color, style, or to make a custom order.
